Redstone Wire

Version 1

Note: Redstone wire doesn't function exactly as it does in Minecraft but demonstrates the main feature of signal propagation across the wire.

Description

Shows redstone components signal traveling across the wire and staying on for as long as the signal is on.

Parts Used

  • Resistors (x5) (100-220 Ω)
  • LEDs (x5)
  • Transistor NPN
  • Transistor PNP

How It Works

The LED voltage is sourced through the power supply rather than the signal. The signal activates the transistors to allow positive voltage to flow through LEDs to turn them on, but turns off as soon as the signal goes low.
